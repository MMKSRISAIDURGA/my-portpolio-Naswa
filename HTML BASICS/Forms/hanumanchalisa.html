Over View of Hanuman Chalisa:

The Hanuman Chalisa is a devotional hymn dedicated to Lord Hanuman, composed by the poetTulsidas in the Awadhi language. It consists of 40 verses that praise Hanuman's strength, bravery, wisdom, and devotion to Lord Rama.Reciting the Hanuman Chalisa is believed to invoke Hanuman's blessings, providing protection, strength, and success. This hymn is widely revered in Hinduism and recited by devotees to seek courage and remove obstacles.
